Gameplay Overview:

Ronin combines elements of platforming and tactical combat in a unique way. The game is divided into two key modes: stealth and action. In stealth mode, you move swiftly through buildings, grappling across rooftops, dodging security cameras, and sneaking past guards. Once enemies detect your presence, the game shifts into a turn-based mode where every move must be calculated. You can pause to plan your next step: whether to deflect bullets with your katana, use your grappling hook to reposition, or strike down enemies in one lethal blow.

The game emphasizes both agility and foresight. Each encounter becomes a puzzle, where timing and positioning are crucial to outmaneuver your opponents. With a limited number of moves, one wrong decision can mean the difference between success and a swift demise.
